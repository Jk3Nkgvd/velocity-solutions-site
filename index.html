Common Questions

How quickly does Velocity respond?

Every lead receives a personalized text response in under 60 seconds, 24/7/365. Your practice is the first one they hear from.

Does it work after hours?

Yes. Velocity is active when your team is sleeping, ensuring weekend and late-night leads are captured, qualified, and moved toward a booking instantly.

Will it replace my staff?

No. Velocity handles the repetitive first conversations and qualification so your front desk can focus on patients in the room. Your team stays in control—every booking is confirmed by a human on your staff.

Does it book directly into my practice management software?

Velocity captures the booking request and places it in your team's confirmation queue—your front desk approves every appointment in one click, so your calendar stays fully under your control. Direct PMS integration is on our roadmap.

Is patient data safe?

Velocity runs on BAA-covered infrastructure with encrypted storage and strict per-practice data isolation. Every conversation is logged and auditable, and SMS consent and opt-outs are handled automatically.

What does onboarding look like?

Days, not months. We configure your qualification criteria and connect your lead sources—there's no software for your team to install. Your front desk gets one dashboard and a booking queue.
